Main Information (‡§Æ‡•Å‡§ñ‡•ç‡§Ø ‡§ú‡§æ‡§®‡§ï‡§æ‡§∞‡•Ä)

Hindu mythology is one of the oldest and most complex mythological traditions in the world. It forms the spiritual foundation of Hinduism, the third-largest religion globally. These stories are not only religious texts but also contain philosophy, ethics, science, and cosmology.
They describe:

  • How the universe was created,
  • How it will be destroyed,
  • How humans should live their lives
  • And the continuous struggle between good and evil.
